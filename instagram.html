Explore Instagram Insights

Instagram is a free social media app owned by Meta (formerly Facebook) that allows users to share photos and videos with their followers. Launched in 2010, it has become one of the most popular platforms worldwide, with over 2 billion users. The app focuses on visual content, offering features like the main feed for posts, Stories that disappear after 24 hours, Reels for short-form vertical videos, and the Explore page to discover trending content. Users can also send private messages, go live, and even shop directly through the app. Instagram is widely used by individuals, influencers, and businesses for personal expression, entertainment, networking, and marketing.

Which age groups use Instagram the most?
Instagram is most popular among younger audiences. The majority of users are aged between 18 and 24 years old, with 31.7% of internet users in this age group using Instagram regularly.
How many hours do people spend on Instagram?
On average, users spend around 30 minutes to 1 hour per day on Instagram. However, for younger demographics, this time can increase significantly, sometimes exceeding 2 hours per day.
What is the impact of Instagram on different age groups?
Instagram has a major impact on social behavior, particularly among teens and young adults. It influences trends, lifestyles, and consumer behavior, with users spending more time consuming media and interacting with brands.
What is the most used feature on Instagram?
Stories are the most used feature on Instagram. With over 500 million users engaging with Instagram Stories daily, this feature has become the cornerstone of Instagram's interactive content.
